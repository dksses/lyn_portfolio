Welcome...

Tasman International Academies (TIA) extends a warm welcome to you and invites you to join the TIA family. TIA's staff members come from a range of cultures so we really understand the needs of international students living and studying in a foreign country. We are a New Zealand Qualifications Authority (NZQA) registered tertiary education provider and a signatory to the Code of Practice for the Pastoral Care of International Students so you can be sure that TIA will provide you with all the proper care and attention you will need during your time here..
