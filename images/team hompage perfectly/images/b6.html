National Diplomas in Business (Level 5 & Level 6)

The National Diplomas in Business are well established national qualifications respected by employers, polytechnics and universities. The practical nature of the courses means that they are well suited to prepare students for employment while at the same time developing business skills useful for more advanced studies.



National Diploma in Business Level 6

  • One Year
  • 8 modules

Aim of the Course

The aims of the course leading to the National Diploma in Business (Level 6) are to:

  1. Enable students to acquire knowledge and competencies and develop skills in a range of business-related management and leadership activities.
  2. Provide advanced business education in industry related business concepts with technical complexity.
  3. Develop versatile and adaptable graduates equipped either to undertake a range of business activities and specific roles at a high level or to apply for advanced study leading to a higher diploma or degree qualification.

Course Description

The National Diploma in Business (Level 6) recognises a wide range of business-related competencies. People who have achieved this qualification have demonstrated the skills, knowledge, and competence to undertake a range of business activities and specific roles at a high level. These graduates are versatile, adaptable and well equipped to commence a business career or apply for further study to a higher diploma or degree qualification.

The following themes are covered in this course:

  • Marketing
  • Human Resource Management
  • Business Relationships
  • International Marketing
  • Business Operations

Future Opportunities

People who have achieved this qualification have demonstrated the skills, knowledge, and competence to undertake a range of business activities and specific roles at a high level including starting their own business. Graduates will also be positioned to apply for advanced study at diploma and degree levels.

Intake

There are eight major modules each represented by a business theme. Students must successfully complete all eight modules to gain the qualification. The course requires one year of full-time study. There are five terms per year. Students may start the course at the beginning of any of these terms and at the middle point of the longer terms.


Entry Requirements

Applicants must have:

  • National Diploma in Business Level 5 from Tasman International Academies or any other accredited provider
  • or an equivalent qualification subject to approval from the Academic Director or Principal
  • or a Year 12 or Year 13 international qualification equivalent to or higher than one of the above

AND

International students for whom English is not their first language must have either:

  • a minimum IELTS (Academic) score of 6.0 with no band score lower than 5.5
  • ORa minimum Tasman English Proficiency Test score of 6.0 with no band lower than 5.5
