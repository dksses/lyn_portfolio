WHY DO YOU CHOOSE TASMAN?

    International Recognition

    As a New Zealand Qualifications Authority (NZQA) registered tertiary institute we offer a variety of qualifications to suit your needs. Our courses are recognised both in New Zealand and internationally. Our courses can lead to future study at education providers in New Zealand, Australia, and the United Kingdom.

    Modern Facilities

    Tasman's premises are modern, light and airy with fantastic views over Auckland's key landmarks. Our spacious classrooms are fully equipped with everything you need for successful study. Students have free fast internet, magazines, big comfy seating, and a student kitchen and canteen area.

    Industry Relationships

    Tasman prides itself on its good relationships with employers. We are privileged to provide students with inspirational talks and seminars from leaders in the field. In some programmes, students have the opportunity to gain work experience through internships with our industry partners.

    Social Activities

    Tasman offers fun outside activities that give students the opportunity to make friends outside the classroom and explore New Zealand at the same time.In the past our activities have included beach barbecues, sports days, the Maritime museum, ice-skating, and a trip to the International Gardens in Hamilton.

Tasman's Expert and Caring Staff


Do you want to study with us? Need more information? Contact one of our friendly staff today!

At TIA, we come from a variety of countries ourselves so we understand the difficulties international students face when coming to live and study in a foreign country. All of our counselling staff are qualified, experienced and bilingual and can offer guidance and support in your own language.
All diploma teachers have extensive experience in their related industries.
